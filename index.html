"IniciaTec, tu camino al futuro"

IniciaTec is a training program, created by Accenture Argentina, whose objective is the development of technical and socio-emotional skills aligned to the digital economy. Associated with Eidos and MindHub, the project seeks to train 100 people in different topics related to technology areas, such as Web Development - Front End, Testing and Monitoring Services, in an environment that emulates an agile, innovative and collaborative system. That is why the program will be aimed at training socio-emotional skills, being an approach to the current work culture.
